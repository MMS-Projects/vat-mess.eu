--- layout: default title: VAT-MESS --- # VAT-MESS This webpage's intented function is providing information about the new EU law known as VAT-MOSS, also known as VAT-MESS. The goal is to provide business owners around the EU with easy understandable information about the law. ## What is VAT-MOSS? VAT-MOSS is a law passed by the EU for using the VAT of the country a product gets bought in. MOSS stands for "Mini One Stop Shop"
